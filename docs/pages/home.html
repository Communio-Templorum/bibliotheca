We are the Main Library of the Communio Templorum. We are working to collect important books and make them available for all members to share.

Our Collections

Libri Sancti

Libri Sancti — our collection of sacred religious texts of the Religio Romana, the Religion of Ancient Rome. These works are of historical and theological importance.

Religio Domestica

The religion of the home, the best place to start if you are new to the Religio Romana.

  • In-depth exploration of the rites and rituals for the house.
  • Discussion on the Gods and how to properly pay our respect.
    • the Lares
    • the Penates
    • the Genius
    • Juno
    • Vesta
  • An overview of the Religio Romana for the practitioner.

Cuneiform Text Corpus

Cuneiform Text Corpus — archive of ancient texts written in cuneiform, the earliest written records and religious stories. Languages include Sumerian and Akkadian.

Scriptorium

We work to preserve ancient texts documenting the history of Rome, the Religio Romana, and the Roman peoples.
